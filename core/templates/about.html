About Us

Welcome to Government Graduate College Jhang

Established in 1926, our institution stands as one of Punjab's most prestigious government colleges with a rich academic heritage. Affiliated with the University of the Punjab, we have been shaping minds and building futures for nearly a century.

Our college takes immense pride in being the alma mater of two Nobel laureates - Dr. Abdus Salam (Physics) and Dr. Har Gobind Khorana (Medicine) - along with numerous other distinguished scholars, poets, and writers who have made significant contributions to their fields.
