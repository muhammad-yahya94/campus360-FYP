Admission Eligibility Criteria

BS Programs Admission Requirements
  • Minimum 45% aggregate marks in FA/F.Sc
  • Minimum 60% marks in relevant subjects (for some programs)
  • Maximum age limit of 26 years may apply
  • Original academic certificates and transcripts required
  • Domicile certificate of Punjab province
MS Programs Admission Requirements
  • Minimum CGPA of 2.5 on a 4.0 scale (or equivalent)
  • Four-year BS degree from HEC recognized institution
  • Preferably in science/engineering discipline
  • Program-specific requirements may apply
  • Research proposal may be required for some programs
Important Notes
  • Check the college website for most up-to-date information
  • Ensure your previous institution is recognized by HEC
  • Eligibility criteria may vary by specific program
  • Admission test/interview may be required for some programs
  • Submit all required documents before deadline
