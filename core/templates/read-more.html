Annual Science Exhibition 2025

April 25, 2025

Main Auditorium, eLEARNING Campus

The Annual Science Exhibition 2025 at eLEARNING brought together brilliant young minds from various departments to showcase innovative projects and experiments. The event was graced by notable alumni and industry experts who judged and appreciated the efforts of the students.

Highlights:
  • 50+ Student Projects Displayed
  • Guest Lecture by Dr. Sarah Malik (NASA)
  • Live Demonstrations and Robotics Show

The event concluded with award distribution and a motivational talk on "Innovation & AI in Education." We are proud of our students and faculty who worked tirelessly to make this event a success.
